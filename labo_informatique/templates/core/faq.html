Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join your laboratory?

Several pathways allow you to join our laboratory as a student, researcher, or collaborator:

  • Participate in events organized or supported by the laboratory (e.g.: hackathons, seminars, workshops)
  • Engage in a joint research project with one of our teams
  • Be recommended by a laboratory member or institutional partner
  • Respond to a call for applications or internship opportunity posted on our site

We invite you to follow our news so you don't miss opportunities to join us.

Do you offer internships for students?

Yes, we regularly welcome interns at all levels (bachelor's, master's, doctorate). Our internships allow students to:

  • Work on concrete and innovative projects
  • Be supervised by experienced researchers
  • Develop advanced technical skills
  • Potentially lead to a thesis or employment

Check our "Join the team" page for current offers or contact us for specific opportunities.

What are your main research areas?

Our main research areas include:

Artificial Intelligence

Deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision

Cybersecurity

Cryptography, intrusion detection, application security

Distributed Systems

Cloud computing, edge computing, blockchain technologies

Software Development

DevOps, software architecture, code quality

For more details, check our About page.

Can we visit your laboratory?

We regularly organize events for the public:

  • Open days: Twice a year (usually in March and October)
  • Guided tours: By appointment for groups
  • Public conferences: Check our events calendar

Contact us to schedule a visit or to know the dates of our upcoming public events.
