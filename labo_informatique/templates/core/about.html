History

The Advanced Computer Science Research Laboratory (Beta), led by Professor Tchana Alain, is a space dedicated to research and innovation in computer science in Cameroon. Our mission is to train high-level researchers and developers, capable of meeting the technological and scientific challenges of the African continent.

The Beta laboratory offers an environment conducive to introduction to research and innovation, with a team of experienced researchers.
